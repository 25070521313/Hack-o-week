Jaipur Trip

Jaipur is known as the Pink City and is famous for its royal palaces, forts, and colorful markets. I explored Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, and enjoyed traditional Rajasthani food.

Goa Vacation

Goa is full of life with beautiful beaches, music, and nightlife. I enjoyed the sea breeze, water sports, and relaxing sunsets.

Visit to Ujjain

Ujjain is a holy city famous for Mahakaleshwar Temple. The spiritual atmosphere and peaceful ghats made the trip truly divine.

Omkareshwar Journey

Omkareshwar is a sacred island shaped like ॐ. The temple, river views, and calm surroundings made it a very peaceful experience.

Mumbai Trip

Mumbai is the city of dreams. I visited Marine Drive, Gateway of India, and experienced the fast life and amazing street food.
